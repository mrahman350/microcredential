Let’s Rock and Roll! Introducing ONE
Rocky Road!

Hit the road with ONE.

When the road gets tough, the tough get sweet. This limited-edition ONE Bar flavor proves that it’s possible to stay sweet on any tough road. ONE Rocky Road flavored bars are the perfect portable snack that is ready for any path you take with a great taste of sumptuous chocolate, tasty almonds, and gooey marshmallows. They feature our 20 grams of protein and 1 gram of sugar, and as with all ONE products, Rocky Road flavored bars are made with delicious, gluten-free ingredients that are totally road trip approved.

Can you imagine digging into a
delicious bowl of rocky road ice
cream after a long, hot road trip?

Yeah, we can too… and with our Rocky Road flavored bars, that fantasy has pretty much become a reality. You’ll love enjoying that chocolatey rocky road flavor any time you need a delicious protein-packed snack. These bars will take you to those dreamy, summer road trip days where all that matters is a good playlist and even better snack – without all the sugar, that is. With a delicious chocolate center filled with almonds and marshmallows, surrounded by a decadent icing-like coating, you’ll forget all about the protein you’re fueling up with. ONE Rocky Road Bars are available now at our website and Amazon, as well as in-store at specialty retailers, such as Vitamin Shoppe, Circle K and more.
